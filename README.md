# Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance using the Superstore dataset. The goal is to uncover insights into revenue trends, profitability, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

# Business Objectives

Evaluate overall sales and profit performance

Identify high-performing and underperforming product categories

Analyze regional contribution to revenue

Track year-over-year growth trends

Detect loss-making products and discount impact
## Key Insights

 1. Revenue Trends showed steady growth from 2014 to 2017, followed by a decline towards 2019
Indicates potential market saturation or operational inefficiencies

 2. Regional Performance
West region generated the highest revenue
South region underperformed consistently

 3. Profitability Analysis
Technology category drives the highest revenue and profit
Several products generate negative profit despite strong sales, largely due to high discounts

 4. Loss Drivers
Significant portion of revenue (~$468K) comes from loss-making transactions
Highlights poor pricing or discount strategy

 5. Year-over-Year Trends
Strong growth observed in early years (2015–2016)
Negative growth recorded in later years (2018–2019)
